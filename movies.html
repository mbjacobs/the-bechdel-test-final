Movies that pass the test:

The Princess Diaries

2001


Director: Garry Marshall

Summary: Shy teenager Mia Thermopolis (Anne Hathaway) is thrown for a loop when, out of the blue, she learns the astonishing news that she's a real-life princess.

Easy A

2010


Director: Will Gluck

Summary: A clean-cut high school student relies on the school's rumor mill to advance her social and financial standing.

Soul Surfer

2011


Director: Sean McNamara

Summary: Surfer Bethany Hamilton overcomes the odds and her fears of returning to the water after losing her arm to a shark.

Bird Box

2018


Director: Susanne Bier

Summary: Five years after an ominous unseen presence drives most of society to suicide, a mother and her children try to reach safety.

A Star is Born

2018


Director: Bradley Cooper

Summary: A musician helps a young singer find fame as age and alcoholism send his own career into a downward spiral.

Captain Marvel

2019


Director: Anna Boden, Ryan Fleck

Summary: Carol Danvers becomes one of the universe's most powerful heroes when Earth is caught in a galactic war between two alien races.

The Devil Wears Prada

2006


Director: David Frankel

Summary: A smart but sensible new graduate lands a job as an assistant to Miranda Priestly, the demanding editor-in-chief of a high fashion magazine.

Black Panther

2018


Director: Ryan Coogler

Summary: T'Challa, heir to the hidden but advanced kingdom of Wakanda, must step forward to lead his people into a new future and must confront a challenger from his country's past.

Ralph Breaks The Internet

2018


Director: Phil Johnston, Rich Moore

Summary: Six years after the events of "Wreck-It Ralph," Ralph and Vanellope, now friends, discover a wi-fi router in their arcade, leading them into a new adventure.
